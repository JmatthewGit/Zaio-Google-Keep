Simple guide to Zaio-Google-Keep

In order to take a note simply click on the "Take a note" textbox.
This will open the note to be edited. You can include a title and the note, then when you wish to save the note simply click outside of the note container.
Your new saved note will appear bellow the "Take a note" textbox.
You can click the textbox again to make a new seperate note.
To edit an existing note simply hover your cursior over it and click, it will bring up the same type of text prompt as when you made it. 
Simply change whatever you like then save again.
To access the sidebar nav simply hover your cursor over it, this will make it extent outwards providing diffrent options.
When you wish for it to disapear move your cursor out of it and it will retract back, simple as that.
